Built for operational posts

A field reporting workspace for hard-to-reach borders.

BorderReach gives national border agencies a controlled way to publish forms, enroll officers, receive structured reports, review records, and visualize activity even when posts have unreliable connectivity.

Form versioning

Publish controlled updates and keep field devices aligned with the latest approved version.

Review-ready records

Store answers as structured JSON while showing report tables that officers and managers can understand.

Operational maps

Upload boundary layers and plot GPS reports against districts, chiefdoms, posts, or field locations.

External integrations

Forward accepted submissions to downstream systems through REST webhooks and exports.

Standards and templates

Start from proven reporting baselines.

Templates give agencies a disciplined starting point. Each project can be copied, adapted, versioned, and published for field use.

ICAO Doc 9303 immigration template

Machine-readable travel document reporting, VIZ/MRZ checks, document validity, traveller identity, inspection observations, and officer decision fields.

WCO Data Model customs template

Declaration, procedure, goods, HS code, transport, consignor, consignee, duty, inspection result, and seizure workflow fields.

WHO IHR point-of-entry health template

Screening, symptoms, exposure risk, vaccination or prophylaxis document, public health action, referral, and notification fields.

Security incident template

Incident type, severity, detection source, action taken, agency notification, evidence, and follow-up ownership.
